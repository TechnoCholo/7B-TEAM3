Skeletal System

What Is the Skeletal System?

The skeletal system is the framework of the human body. It is made up of bones, cartilage, joints, and ligaments that all work together to support the body.


The skeletal system protects important organs like the brain, heart, and lungs. It also helps the body move by working together with muscles.


An adult human body has 206 bones that provide structure, protection, and support every day.
